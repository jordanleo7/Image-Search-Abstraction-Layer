Image Search Abstraction Layer

Example query usage:

After /search/ enter your search and optional offset.
Example: https://img-search-abstractionlayer.glitch.me/search/lolcats%20funny?offset=2

Go to /latest to see the last 10 searches
Example: https://img-search-abstractionlayer.glitch.me/latest

This site uses Google Custom Search and is limited to 100 free searches per day. If your search returns no results then check the console for "dailyLimitExceeded".
